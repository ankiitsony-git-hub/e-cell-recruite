About Us

SkillBridge is a platform where college students can find real freelance work and startups can hire skilled students. Many students learn useful skills but they rarely get the chance to use them in real projects.

Our mission is to help students gain practical experience during college.

Our vision is to create a simple network where students and startups can work together.

Problem Statement

The Problem

Students learn technical skills but companies usually want experienced workers. Because of this, students find it difficult to get their first opportunity.

Who Faces This Problem

Students who want experience and startups that need affordable talent both face this challenge.

Product / Service Solution

SkillBridge allows students to create profiles, list their skills, and apply for projects posted by startups.

Student profiles and portfolios
Freelance project marketplace
Easy communication with clients
Ratings and feedback

How It Helps Users

Students get real experience and can earn money. Startups get access to skilled students at reasonable cost.
